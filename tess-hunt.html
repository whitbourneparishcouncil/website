Tess Hunt

Tess Hunt

Councillor, Whitbourne Parish Council

Tess moved to Whitbourne in 2014.

Before retirement she worked in public service and community-facing roles. She has been involved in village organisations including the Playing Fields.

She joined the Parish Council in 2023.
